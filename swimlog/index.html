Swim with joy,
without overdoing it.

SwimLog+ is a lightweight app for logging, reviewing, analyzing, and improving your swim training.
Track distance, stroke sets, lessons, perceived effort, and focus points, then review your progress in a clear and practical way.

主な機能

Core Features

距離記録だけで終わらず、継続・振り返り・フォーム確認までつなげる構成です

More than just distance logging — built to support consistency, review, and technique awareness.

トレーニング記録

Training Log

総距離でも、種目別セットでも記録可能。レッスン日、主観強度（RPE）、気になるポイントも残せます

Log either total distance or stroke-based sets. Also record lesson days, perceived effort (RPE), and focus points.

Distance Sets RPE

Calendar / 振り返り

Calendar & Review

練習日・日別記録・週間サマリーを確認。CSV出力にも対応し、継続の見える化を強力にサポートします

Review swim days, daily records, and weekly summaries. Export training data via CSV when needed.

Calendar Weekly Summary CSV

Insights / フォーム動画

Insights & Form Video

総泳距離、継続日数、最長距離、楽に泳げる距離、RPE傾向などを確認。フォーム動画の再生・比較にも対応

Track total distance, streaks, longest swim, comfort distance, and effort trends. Store and compare form videos.

Pro Features

For swimmers who want deeper analysis, Pro unlocks monthly insights, detailed CSV export, and enhanced form comparison.

Monthly Analysis

Review monthly totals, swim days, stroke breakdown, and daily charts in one place.
